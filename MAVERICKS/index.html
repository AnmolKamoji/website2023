About Us

Who are we? We are the mavericks team of FRCRCE( bandra west). We are a technical team that works on the development of drones and other aerial vechiles. The team focuses on creating UAV's that are better, cost effective and efficient than the other UAV's available in the market. We take part in different national and international competitions as a way to test our abilities. Our motto is very simple 'To do the best we can do.' With each competition that we take part in, we try to bring in something new to the world of aerial flight systems. The major advantage that the team has is that we are very hardworking. There is genuine understanding as well as cooperation within the team and at the same time a sense of responsibility.
